The Internet of Everything is creating tremendous opportunities and challenges for organizations. Follow the journey and see how you can capture your share of the value of connecting people, process, data, and things.

Robert is the CIO of a successful software company offering solutions for retailers such as business analysis, inventory systems, field service application, and mobile application to assist shoppers.

Meet Robert.

It’s 8am and he is on a conference call with the executive team in charge of the most recent blockbuster product.

They are reporting much higher than expected usage and need to bring new resources on-line as soon as possible before the response times degrade too much. Robert verifies with his team that network, data center and security infrastructure resources are being automatically provisioned and coordinated to support the additional traffic.

See the Technology

CIOs need an agile infrastructure that can rapidly and cost efficiently provide network, compute, storage, and security resources to users and customers alike.

Cisco Application-Centric Infrastructure

Helps Robert transforms next-generation data center and cloud deployments with innovative Application Centric Infrastructure (ACI) that radically simplifies, optimizes and accelerates the entire application deployment lifecycle.

The Cisco Application Policy Infrastructure Controller (APIC) is the unified point of automation and management for the Cisco ACI fabric, policy enforcement, and health monitoring. The Cisco Nexus 9000 Series offers Gigabit Ethernet switch configurations that can take full advantage of Cisco ACI application policy–based services and infrastructure automation features.

Cisco Prime Network Services Controller

Allows Robert to address the challenge of making frequent changes to complex networks by automating processes, freeing staff to focus on optimizing the network environment.

Data Center environments can consist of thousands of virtual services elements, such as firewalls, load balancers, routers, and switches. Cisco Prime Network Services Controller simplifies operations with centralized, automated multi-device and policy management.
